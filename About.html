About:

The Dark Angels, or TDA, is an esports team based in Europe.

We currently compete in the games Fortnite & Gorilla Tag.

We were originally founded in 2024 by our founder, Meeko.

Our goals:

We aim to build a competitive gaming organisation that can compete with the best on the biggest stages. So far, we haven't competed in tournaments, but we're looking to expand our team so that we can compete in large tournaments.
